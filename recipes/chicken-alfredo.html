Chicken Alfredo

Description

This creamy chicken alfredo pasta dish is a very easy yet tasty food to cook for your next meal.

A perfect begginer dish that doesn't require you to become Gordan Ramsey to prepare!

Ingredients

  • 2 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 3 cloves of minced garlic
  • 2 cups heavy cream
  • 1 cup grated Parmeson cheese
  • 8 oz fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • Optionally you can add chopped parsley for garnish

Steps

  1. Bring a pot of salted water to a boil and cook the fettuccine according to package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Season the chicken breasts with salt and pepper on both sides.
  3. Heat olive oil in a large pan over medium heat and cook the chicken until golden and fully cooked (about 6–7 minutes per side). Remove and slice into strips.
  4. In the same pan, melt the butter and sauté the minced garlic for about 1 minute until fragrant.
  5. Pour in the heavy cream and let it simmer for 3–5 minutes.
  6. Stir in the Parmesan cheese until the sauce thickens.
  7. Add the cooked pasta to the sauce and toss to coat evenly.
  8. Return the sliced chicken to the pan and mix everything together.
  9. Garnish with parsley if desired and serve warm.
