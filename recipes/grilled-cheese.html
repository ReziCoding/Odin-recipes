Grilled Cheese

Description

The classic grilled cheese sandwich is a very tasty meal that takes almost zero work. The melty cheese goodness is perfect for a quick and easy meal that is affordable!

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 slices of cheese
  • 1-2 tablespoons butter

Steps

  1. Heat a pan over medium heat.
  2. Spread butter on one side of each slice of bread.
  3. Place one slice of bread in the pan, butter side down.
  4. Add the cheese slices on top of the bread in the pan.
  5. Place the second slice of bread on top, butter side up.
  6. Cook until the bottom slice is golden brown.
  7. Flip the sandwich carefully and cook the other side until golden brown and the cheese is melted.
  8. Remove from the pan and serve warm.
